About Us

Mission Statement

To equip east-of-Bayshore youth to grow spiritually, gain life skills and develop as leaders so that they have hope and a future.

History

Hope Horizon is a non-profit organization that aims to help the younger generation through education and faith. The organization's history includes the establishment of a Christian community in East Palo Alto and the incorporation of Bayshore Christian Ministries (BCM) as a non-profit.
